Floating Therapy

Floating therapy is a deeply restorative practice where you float effortlessly in warm, mineral-rich water designed to reduce external stimulation.

The water is saturated with Epsom salt, creating natural buoyancy so your body can fully relax without effort. Heated to skin temperature, the experience gently dissolves the boundary between body and water, allowing the nervous system to slow down and reset.

It’s a quiet space for rest, recovery, and mental clarity.

A Calm State of Being

By minimizing sensory input, floating can guide the body into a naturally relaxed state. Many people leave a session feeling lighter, clearer, and more grounded.

Physical Benefits

  • Relieves tension in muscles and joints
  • Supports natural recovery after physical activity
  • Encourages the release of endorphins
  • Promotes deep physical relaxation

Mental & Emotional Benefits

  • Stress and anxiety reduction
  • Improved sleep quality
  • Creative flow and clarity
  • A deeper sense of presence
