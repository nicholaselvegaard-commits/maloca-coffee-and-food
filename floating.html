Floating Therapy

Floating therapy is a deeply restorative practice where you float effortlessly in warm, mineral-rich water designed to reduce external stimulation.

Heated to skin temperature and saturated with Epsom salt, the body enters a state of deep calm, allowing the nervous system to reset.

  • Deep relaxation
  • Improved sleep
  • Reduced stress and anxiety
  • Mental clarity
