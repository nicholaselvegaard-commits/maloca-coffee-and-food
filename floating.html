Floating Therapy

Floating therapy is a deeply restorative practice where you float effortlessly in warm, mineral-rich water designed to reduce external stimulation.

Saturated with Epsom salt and heated to skin temperature, the water allows the nervous system to slow down and reset.

It is a quiet space for rest, recovery, and mental clarity.

Benefits

  • Deep physical relaxation
  • Improved sleep quality
  • Stress and anxiety reduction
  • Muscle and joint relief
  • Mental clarity and calm focus
